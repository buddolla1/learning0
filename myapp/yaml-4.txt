Developer writes an interceptor in Java.

Best Practices Analysis automatically checks code on save or edit:

Are preHandle, postHandle, and afterCompletion used correctly?

The agent highlights warnings and suggests fixes.

Workflow speeds up development and enforces best practices automatically.
